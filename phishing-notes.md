# Phishing Awareness Notes

## What is Phishing?
Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information via fake emails or websites.

## Common Techniques
- **Spear Phishing**: Targeted attacks on individuals.
- **Whaling**: Targeting high-profile executives.
- **Vishing**: Voice-based phishing over phone.

## Prevention Tips
1. Verify email senders.
2. Avoid clicking unknown links.
3. Use two-factor authentication (2FA).
4. Educate users through training.
